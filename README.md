## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing mobile sales data using **Power BI** to uncover key business insights such as revenue trends, brand performance, and customer behavior.

The dashboard provides a clear and interactive way to explore sales data across different dimensions like **time, location, and payment methods**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze total revenue and sales performance
* Identify top-performing mobile brands and models
* Understand monthly sales trends
* Analyze customer ratings and satisfaction
* Explore payment method usage

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains the following key fields:

* **Brand** – Mobile brand name
* **Model** – Specific mobile model
* **Country** – Sales location
* **Price_USD** – Price of the product
* **Units_Sold** – Number of units sold
* **Revenue_USD** – Total revenue generated
* **Customer_Rating** – Customer feedback rating
* **Payment_Method** – Mode of payment
* **Sale_Month / Sale_Year** – Time of sale

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Metrics (KPIs)

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Units Sold
* Average Customer Rating

### 🔹 Visualizations

* 📈 Revenue Trend by Month
* 🌍 Revenue by Country (Map)
* 💳 Payment Method Distribution
* 📊 Units Sold by Brand
* 📋 Top Selling Models Table

### 🔹 Filters (Slicers)

* Brand
* Country
* Month

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Certain brands generate significantly higher revenue than others
* Sales show fluctuations across different months
* Credit Card and Online Payments are commonly used
* Some countries contribute more to overall revenue
* High-rated products tend to perform better in sales
